Firestone, Colorado

Population 2021: 18,813

Firestone is a city located in Colorado. With a 2020 population of 18,813, it is the 34th largest city in Colorado and the 1950th largest city in the United States. Firestone is currently growing at a rate of 7.53% annually and its population has increased by 85.40% since the most recent census, which recorded a population of 10,147 in 2010. Firestone reached it's highest population of 18,813 in 2021. Spanning over 14 miles, Firestone has a population density of 1,393 people per square mile.
The average household income in Firestone is $109,596 with a poverty rate of 3.42%. The median rental costs in recent years comes to $1,545 per month, and the median house value is $354,000. The median age in Firestone is 35.3 years, 34.9 years for males, and 36 years for females. For every 100 females there are 103.1 males.
