Target Audience

My target audience is ages 15-65, anyone who has the capability of using technology with a need to know the forecasted weather in their area. They are individuals who may want to know the weather in a specific area they plan to visit so they can plan accordingly, as well as those who would likek to have quick access to the weather on the go or at home.

Two Personas

Kylie commutes to work on a daily basis from Erie to Denver. She enjoys her life at home with her husband and growing kids. Aa a family, they enjoy taking roadtrips to visit their family in other states.

Collin is very technologically savvy, and he enjoys his work in the office. When he is not working, he enjoys spending his free time mountain biking and embarking on backpacking adventures. As a single guy, he is always up for taking road trips with his buddies.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Living in colorado provides much beautiful mountain scenery, but the weather is not always cooperative. Kylie needs to be prepared to leave for her commute earlier on days that the weather is not so aggreeable. Kylie can plan ahead by viewing the weekly forecast to determine an appropriate departure time.

Scenario 2: Collin uses his smartphone on a daily basis to track the weather to help determine his next outing, he views the forecast early on in the week so that he can plan for his outdoor adventures.

Scenario 3: Kylie and her family are planning to visit relatives in Northern Idaho, they can search the weather patterns in that area to determine what kind of weather they should be packing for.

Scenario 4: Collin and his buddies made a spur of the moment decision to road-trip to Utah for a Mountain-biking trip, he has access to view the weather on the go using his smartphone so he can quickly pack for his trip.

Scenario 5: Kylie is planning a family outing to the lake, but the last time they went, they were rained out. She is planning ahead by checking the weather for any chance of upcoming rain.
